Customizer Panel Overlay

A JS-driven frontend panel with live preview — how should it overlay the page?

A

Slide-Out Drawer

Panel slides in from the right edge, pushing content or overlaying it. Triggered by a floating button.

B

Floating Toolbar

Thin persistent bar at top or bottom of page with icons for quick edits. Expands into full panel on click.

C

Modal Overlay

Full-screen dimmed overlay with centered editing panel. Best focus, blocks page interaction while editing.
